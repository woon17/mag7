This dashboard explores layoffs, stock performance, and capital expenditure across the Magnificent 7 tech companies from 2020 to 2025.

🖱️

Using the Dashboard

  • Filter Bar (top right) — toggle companies and years to focus your view
  • Timeline Scrubber (bottom) — hover to move the time cursor; see live stock prices and layoff markers
  • Compare Mode — drag across the chart area to select a date range and compare returns
  • Normalize — switch between raw price, vs S&P 500, or vs Nasdaq-100
  • Layoff Cards — cards on the right highlight events near the current date
  • Legend items — click any company name to show/hide its line
📊

Static Visualisations
